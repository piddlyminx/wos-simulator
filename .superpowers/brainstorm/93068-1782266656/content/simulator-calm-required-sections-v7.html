Why this is calmer

  • Required setup is visible as sections, so users know what matters.
  • Only one section is expanded at a time on mobile.
  • Each collapsed section shows enough summary to catch mistakes.
  • The stat matrix still exists, but appears in a focused editor instead of permanently occupying the page.
  • Buffs remain last and folded because they are optional compared with troops, heroes, stats, and joiners.
